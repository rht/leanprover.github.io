Jeremy Avigad (Oct 16 2019 at 12:05):

I'd like to remind everyone that Rob Lewis and I are organizing a meeting at Carnegie Mellon from Monday, January 6 to Friday, January 10: http://www.andrew.cmu.edu/user/avigad/meetings/fomm2020/. Monday to Wednesday will feature invited speakers on a range of topics (with a break on Wednesday afternoon), and Thursday and Friday will be a Lean workshop.

Rob and I are starting to think about how to block out the time for Thursday and Friday. Here are some things we want to fit in:
- A presentation on Lean 4 from Sebastian Ullrich and a subsequent discussion.
- A discussion of plans for mathlib, as well as infrastructure and tools for maintaining it.
- A discussion of the Formal Abstracts project.
- Short, informal presentations of recent work and work in progress.
If any of you have other suggestions for things we should budget time for, please let us know.
